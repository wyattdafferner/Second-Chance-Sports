    We collect our equipment by pairing with youth sports organizations in more affluent areas. We collect equipment in bins that we place out during various sports events like tryouts, season kickoffs, or tournaments. We only except gently used equipment that is ready to get back in the game. We accept equipment for sports like baseball, softball, and soccer & possibly more in the future.
