    Helping All Kids Kids Play Ball

    At Second Chance Sports, we work to get all kids off the bench so they can experience the thrill of the game. We do this by providing young athletes across many sports with the equipment they need to play.

    As the price to play and stay with sports continues to surge, Second Chance Sports will cover the cost of equipment to help young athletes stay off the sidelines. We know how it important it is to be included on the team, and we will fight to support this cause.
